Featured Courses

The Faculty of Science offers diverse courses in biological, physical, and mathematical sciences to foster analytical thinking. Core disciplines include chemistry, physics, and biology, alongside high-demand modern fields such as data science, biotechnology, and environmental conservation. Students can typically choose between three-year general degrees or four-year honors programs that provide in-depth specialization for research-focused careers.
